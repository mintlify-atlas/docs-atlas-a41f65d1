Megatron-LM and Megatron Core are NVIDIA's open-source frameworks for training large language models at scale. **Megatron Core** provides composable, GPU-optimized building blocks for custom training pipelines. **Megatron-LM** is a batteries-included reference implementation with pre-configured training scripts for GPT, LLaMA, Mixtral, and more.

## Performance at scale

Megatron achieves up to **47% Model FLOP Utilization (MFU)** on H100 clusters, training 462B parameter models across 6,144 GPUs with superlinear weak scaling.
